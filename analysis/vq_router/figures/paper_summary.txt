DiMoR VQ Router — Mechanism Analysis Summary
==================================================

Finding 1: Codebook is HEALTHY
  10 template graphs are highly diverse (avg cosine < 0.08).
  No codebook collapse detected.

Finding 2: Routing is LAYER-STATIC
  5/6 layers always select the same template regardless of time.
  1/6 layers show time-varying template selection.
  Layers choose DIFFERENT templates from each other.

Finding 3: Gain is from DEPTH-WISE CAPACITY, not temporal adaptation
  Different layers specialize to different graph topologies.
  This explains why VQ Router improves short-term prediction
  but does not provide strongly time-varying dynamic graphs.
